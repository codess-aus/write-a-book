# Book Outline Wizard

**Your guided companion for creating a professional book outline**

Welcome to the Book Outline Wizard — a step-by-step tool designed to help new technical authors
structure their book ideas into a complete, publisher-ready outline. Work through each section at
your own pace, save your progress automatically, and export everything as formatted Markdown files
when you're done.

## How It Works

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 📝 **Fill in each section** | The wizard walks you through seven key sections, from your author bio to a detailed chapter-by-chapter breakdown. |
| 💾 **Save as you go** | Your progress is saved automatically in your browser — come back any time without losing your work. |
| 📦 **Export when ready** | Download all completed sections as a ZIP archive of Markdown files, ready to share with your editor or publisher. |
| 🔄 **Revise freely** | Navigate back to any section to refine your answers before exporting the final outline. |

## What You Will Create

The wizard produces a set of Markdown files that together form your complete book outline:

- **Author bio** — who you are and your expertise
- **Book goal** — title, subtitle, audience, and unique selling point
- **Competitive analysis** — how your book compares to existing titles
- **Learning outcomes** — what readers will gain
- **Book structure** — parts and chapters
- **Detailed chapter outlines** — descriptions, page counts, and sub-headings for every chapter
- **Community outreach** — technical reviewers, Amazon reviewers, and influencers *(optional)*
